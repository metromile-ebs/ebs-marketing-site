DFNOL — Dynamic First Notice of Loss

Delight your customers with the highest rated digital FNOL experience and capture rich, structured data for downstream processing.

The key to success with AI begins with FNOL. The First Notice of Loss is an insurer’s biggest opportunity to impress, thereby building trust and loyalty; or to disappoint, leading them to tell the world about their negative experience. FNOL is also an opportunity to capture rich structured information directly from the customer. This reduces downstream processing costs, supplies our AI more data to identify fraud early, and streamline downstream claims processing activity. Metromile’s DFNOL provides a streamlined, delightful experience that experience-conscious consumers demand. The result is happier customers, fewer phone calls, and a flood of rich data to help make your claims organization even more efficient. Learn more.
