The Claims AI Platform

Report

Improve customer experience and downstream processing.

The secret to success with AI lies in the ability to gather a mountain of rich, reliable data through a customer’s first notice of loss. Report was designed to feed the right data to our Claims AI to enable better fraud detection with Detect, and increase opportunities for automation with Streamline — all without changing your existing business process. On top of that, it was rated #1 in user experience from Forrester Research.

Detect

Reduce loss leakage and increase SIU efficiency.

Upgrade your rules-based fraud detection with Detect, a truly AI-powered fraud detection tool powered by our Claims AI which has been trained by some of the smartest data scientists in the industry. Scan every incoming claim in seconds to help your team understand why each prediction was made, while overriding predictions with the expertise and constraints unique to your business. When paired with Report, you’ll catch more real fraud, and fewer claims that only look like fraud.

Replay

Replay incidents to catch more fraud.

Equip your SIU team with the ability to virtually replay the details of an incident from any kind of telematics data. Your SIUs will feel that they have superpowers with Replay, which will let them investigate incidents as if they were there. Corroborate the details of loss with details of the trip to uncover inconsistencies. Paired with Detect, your SIUs will uncover significantly more fraud, faster and easier.

Streamline

Increase efficiency and improve employee experience.

Supercharge your claims organization and cut LAE with Streamline, an AI-powered automation suite that leaves RPA in the dust. Free your claims handlers from menial, repetitive tasks so they can spend time providing superior customer service, decreasing the risk of a bad experience damaging your brand. Unlock progressively higher levels of efficiency when you pair Streamline with any of the products in the Claims AI Platform.
