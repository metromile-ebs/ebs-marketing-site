Dynamic First Notice of Loss (DFNOL)

The key to success with AI begins with FNOL. The point of loss is an insurer’s biggest opportunity to impress a customer, which builds trust and loyalty, or to disappoint, leading those unhappy customers to tell the world. Metromile’s DFNOL provides a streamlined, delightful experience that experience-conscious consumers of today demand. The result is happier customers, fewer phone calls, and a rich tapestry of data that other AI-powered tools in the suite can take advantage of.

Fraud Intelligence

As criminals become more sophisticated, only AI has the ability to reduce your loss leakage without massively scaling your Special Investigative Unit. However, AI can’t do this job alone. Our approach encapsulates the deep expertise of your investigators into signals that our AI power tools take advantage of, leading to happier investigators and drastically reducing your loss ratio. Use as a standalone product, or pair with DFNOL to hamper criminals at the point of loss.

Claims Automation Engine

The real magic of AI is realized with our Claims Automation Engine. More than simple RPA, this tool takes on the high volume of easy, mundane, and repetitive tasks inherent in claims processing. Not only will you drastically reduce LAE, cycle times, and human error, but your claims adjusters will love you. Gone are the days where they must robotically spend most of their time on mundane tasks. Now, they can focus on higher touch customer service for challenging claims.
